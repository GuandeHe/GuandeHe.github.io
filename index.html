Guande He (何冠德)

Master student, TSAIL Group, Tsinghua University.

I’m a final-year master’s student at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jun Zhu and Prof. Jianfei Chen. Before that, I received my B.Eng. from the School of Software, Tsinghua University in 2021.

My research interest includes machine learning and foundation models. I am currently working on the following topics:

  • Uncertainty Quantification and Calibration of Large Language Models.
  • Generative models in Audio Domain.
